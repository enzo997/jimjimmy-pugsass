About us

Hand crafted in York our beautiful marquees bring timeless elegance to your celebration and an atmosphere of luxury to make your party perfect.

Welcome to JimJimmy Marquees

Friends Jim and Jimmy founded JimJimmy Marquees with a shared vision to create an exciting outdoor business built on the values of collaborative and respectful customer service, beautifully made products, sustainability and...fun.

We are committed to offering the very best products supported by the highest levels of service. We are proud to be the exclusive partner of artisan manufacturer Wills Marquees in Southern and South West England.

Hand made in York with painstaking attention to detail our beautiful pole-tents feature breathable canvas canopies and posses a timeless elegance that will bring an evocative aura of luxury to your celebration.
